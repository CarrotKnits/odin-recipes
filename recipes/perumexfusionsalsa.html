Peruvian Mexican fusion salsa

A tasty salsa improvisation I made while in Peru.
I was missing the Mexican salsa that I learned how
to make from my Mexican friends back home in California
and substituted aguaymanto for the tomatillos since
they are in the same family. We don't have tomatillos in
Cusco so this is the best I could do. Also used some of
the local chiles/aji here as it is not easy to find the
ones I am familiar with. Hope you like it! :D
