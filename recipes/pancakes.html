Pancakes

Super simple pancakes that are quick and delicious.
You can even make the batter the night before and store
it in the fridge to make the next morning.

How to make

Ingredients:

Dry:

  • 2 cups AP flour
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda

Wet:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 cups milk (or yogurt, cream, sour cream)
  • 1/2 ts[ vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Combine and mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine and whisk/mix wet
    ingredients well.
  3. Mix wet ingredients into the dry ingredients.
  4. Pour palm sized portions of the batter into a
    lightly oiled/buttered pan on medium heat.
  5. Cooked first side for about 2 minutes and lightly
    browned.
  6. Flip and cooked other side for about 1-2 minutes
    until cooked through.
  7. Repeat until all is cooked, or save some of the
    batter for the next day. Enjoy!
