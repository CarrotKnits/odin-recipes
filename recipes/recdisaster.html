Recipe for Disaster

This will cause not so great things to happen.

Ingredients:

  • phone
  • legos
  • cheese
  • 1/2 a sock
  • water

Instructions:

  1. In a blender add the phone and legos, then blend
    on medium and pulse as needed.
  2. Add water to the mix and blend into a paste.
  3. Strain through 1/2 a sock.
  4. Eat the cheese.
  5. Toss the paste in the trash.
  6. Good-bye phone.
  7. Enjoy! :D
