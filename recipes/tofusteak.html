Tofu Steak With Mushrooms

Delicious and filling! :D

This Tofu Steak is very delicious with tons of
mushrooms of your choice. It's very filling.

This dish is super quick and easy for those busy nights
when you need to relax after a hard day's work.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pkg firsm tofu
  • 50g whatever mushrooms you like
  • 50g whatever other mushrooms you like
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 1/2 dried red chili pepper
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • tiny drizzle of sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Cut the mushrooms.
  2. Chop the garlic.
  3. Heat the tofu in microwave for 1 minute and then remove
    the excess liquid with a paper towel.
  4. On both sides sprinkle on salt, pepper and all purpose
    flour or potato starch.
  5. Add sesame oil to the pan and turn on the burner. Then
    saute the tofu on both sides until lightly browned and
    add to a plate.
  6. In same pan add the garlic and chili pepper, and stir
    until fragrant
  7. Add the mushrooms and saute for a minute.
  8. Add the mirin and soy sauce coating the mushrooms evenly.
  9. Add the mushrooms on top of the tofu steak and enjoy!
