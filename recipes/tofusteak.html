Tofu Steak With Mushrooms

Delicious and filling! :D

This Tofu Steak is very delicious with tons of
mushrooms of your choice. It's very filling.

This dish is super quick and easy for those busy nights
when you need to relax after a hard day's work.

  • 1/2 pkg firsm tofu
  • 50g whatever mushrooms you like
  • 50g whatever other mushrooms you like
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 1/2 dried red chili pepper
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • tiny drizzle of sesame oil
